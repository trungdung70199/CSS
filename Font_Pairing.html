Beautiful Norway

Norway has a total area of 385,252 square kilometers and a population of 5,438,657 (December 2020). Norway is bordered by Sweden, Finland and Russia to the north-east, and the Skagerrak to the south, with Denmark on the othe side.


Norway has beautiful moutains, glaciers and stunnig fjords.Oslo, the capital, is a city of green spaces and museums. Bergen, with colorful wooden house, is the starting point for cruises to the dramic Sognefjord. Norway is also known for fishing, hiking and skiing.
